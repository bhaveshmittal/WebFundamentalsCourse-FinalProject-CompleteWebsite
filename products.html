About the TPS-L2 Walkman

“It sounds like it weighs a ton”

The TPS-L2 was the first commercially available personal stereo cassette player, and now it is available again for you!

Do not depend on the internet connection to listen to your favorite songs.

The walkman produces such a big, rich sound it can only be compared to a very elaborate and expensive component stereo system. Yet, it’s so small you can take it anywhere you go - but, of course, big enough so everybody can see how hype you are with it! There is really no way to convey the remarkable sound quality of this little machine.

There has never been a better way to make bike riding, roller-skating, skiing, or just taking a walk more fun. Because there is no easier way to take your music along for the ride.

And the best feature: it comes with an extra jack for a second set of headphones for you to share your soundtracks with your beloved ones!
